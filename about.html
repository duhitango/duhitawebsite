About Duhita

Duhita is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering women and nurturing futures in North Bengal. Our mission is to provide support, education, and opportunities to women and children in tea gardens and red-light areas of Siliguri.

Founded in [year], Duhita has been working tirelessly to create positive change in our community. Our team of dedicated volunteers and professionals work together to implement various programs and initiatives aimed at improving the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision

A world where every woman and child has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Mission

To empower women and children through education, skill development, and support services, creating a pathway to a brighter future.
